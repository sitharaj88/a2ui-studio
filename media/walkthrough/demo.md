# Zero-setup demos

Open the **Gallery** view and click a card marked **Instant demo** — a prerecorded A2UI v0.9.1 stream renders locally, no AI provider required.

Try the **Trip explorer**: drag the sliders, switch tabs, type into fields — everything is two-way bound to a live data model you can watch (and edit!) in the **Data** inspector tab.

When you click a button in a demo surface, the action round-trips to your configured AI provider, which takes over the conversation.
